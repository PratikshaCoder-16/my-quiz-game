Game Information & Rules

The Programming Quiz consists of three levels of questions. Each level has 10 questions, with the final level containing 5 harder questions.

How to Play:

  1. Login with a valid User ID and Password.
  2. Select your branch.
  3. Answer the questions in each level.
  4. Your score will be displayed after each level.
  5. At the end of the quiz, your total score will be shown along with motivational feedback.
  6. You can choose to restart or close the game after finishing.
